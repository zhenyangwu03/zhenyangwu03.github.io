Bio

Zhenyang Wu is a visual artist based between New York and Shanghai, working across video and photography.

His practice explores surveillance, memory, displacement, and bureaucratic language through layered imagery, abstraction, and visual metaphor.

Drawing from archival materials, found footage, and quotidian gestures, he examines foreignness, repetition, loss, and digital entropy across personal and institutional spaces.
